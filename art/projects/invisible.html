Description

[Based on the documentation images — please confirm/correct:] A series of infrared photographs, rendered in a consistent pink-violet palette, documenting public urban spaces — parks, streets, fences. A recurring motif is a street sign or notice board with its face wiped blank, the information it should carry erased or withheld.

Concept

Infrared photography records light outside the visible spectrum — a literal register of the "invisible." Pairing this with blank signage doubles the theme: what the camera sees that the eye cannot, alongside information that should be public but has been removed. The series reads as a quiet study of withheld or unseen information in shared space.
