Concept

Formication is the medical term for the sensation of insects crawling on or under the skin — a perceptual glitch where the body registers a presence that isn't physically there. The work takes this as a starting point for a wider inquiry into the unreliability of perception, organism, and structure, themes explored further in the Maska interview below.
