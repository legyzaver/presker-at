Title
Dancing with the Corner
Year
2004
Medium
Performance video
Status
Sold — not publicly viewable

Description

A performance video, treated with an edge-detection/outline effect that renders the figure as a glowing white contour against total black. The body extends along the diagonal lines where two walls meet at a corner — limbs stretched outward along each wall — using restricted movement and balance to engage directly with the architecture of the room. The video's own title card confirms the title, 2004 date, and authorship.
