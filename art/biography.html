Miha Presker

Biography

Miha Presker (born 1979, Maribor, Slovenia) is an interdisciplinary artist working across installation, photography, light, and interactive media. After studying mechanical engineering and computer science in Maribor, he continued his education at the University of Applied Arts Vienna, where he studied with Bernhard Leitner and later Erwin Wurm.

His practice combines technological systems with philosophical investigations into perception, projection, and space. Working at the intersection of the physical and the projected, Presker creates situations in which the viewer becomes aware of the mechanics of seeing — the thresholds at which observation becomes surveillance, presence becomes trace, and space becomes image.

His works have been exhibited internationally and include long-term collaborations with artist Luiza Margan. He was awarded the OHO Award in 2007 and participated in the ISCP International Studio and Curatorial Program residency in New York in 2008.

Presker lives and works in Vienna.
