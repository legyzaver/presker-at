Miha Presker
Installation · Light · Photography · Interactive Media

Miha Presker is a Slovenian-born artist based in Vienna whose work investigates the relationship between human perception and machine vision. Through installations, light sculptures, photography and interactive systems, he explores the boundaries between physical space, projected image and technological observation.
